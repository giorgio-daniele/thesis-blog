Linear Servers Overview

The Linear Servers data frame displays information about various linear streaming servers. Each entry includes a canonical name (CNAME) that corresponds to the server and its associated Content Delivery Network (CDN). The CDN is crucial for optimizing the delivery of video content by caching and distributing it closer to users.
