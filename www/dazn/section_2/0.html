DAZN Servers Profile

This page is intended to display the names of DAZN servers, providing an overview of all services involved. It includes primary services, often referred to as linears, which are responsible for the distribution of video content. Additionally, secondary services are highlighted, which are involved in authentication, streaming support, and the loading of images and assets within the application. Finally, the page also covers tertiary services, which focus on telemetry, ensuring that performance and usage data are collected for analysis and improvement. On the left, there is the profile at TCP layer, while on the right there is the UDP layer profile.
